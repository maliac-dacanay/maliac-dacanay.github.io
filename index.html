DISNEYLAND

15 years later, I finally was able to visit Disneyland again Summer 2018

We traveled to Las Vegas, Nevada a few weeks before the KAR dance competition and because we were there early, me, my brother (CJ), my cousin (whom we consider our older sister), and my brother's friend (whom we consider our cousin), all made a daring decision to drive to California the EXACT SAME night we arrive to Las Vegas. It took us about an hour for us to get ready and my brother drove all of us to California .. With ZERO SLEEP whatsoever. We took a few stops to take pictures and to buy food... Of course me and my sister would be the ones paying since thats the most we could do since the boys were the only ones driving. Around 7/8 am, we arrived to Disneyland. Even though CJ and Winston looked like walking zombies, I'm just glad this last minute plan pulled through and in the end, if we all had fun then thats all that matters :)


KAR Dance Competition

My first dance competition out of state Summer 2018

I've been dancing for about 3 years now and I've been looking forward to travel to Vegas for this dance competition. The studio I attended always goes to Vegas every two years to compete at KAR. Was I nervous? Of course I was but it felt so good dancing on a stage with thousands of people watching.
